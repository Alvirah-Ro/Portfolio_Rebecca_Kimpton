About Me

I have a background in music, performing the oboe and English horn for several years with the Waterloo-Cedar Falls Symphony Orchestra. I recently decided to change careers and embarked on my new journey to become a software developer. Along the way, I earned an IBM certificate in full-stack software development. I am excited to use my artistic inclinations and creativity to design and develop attractive and functional apps and webpages.
